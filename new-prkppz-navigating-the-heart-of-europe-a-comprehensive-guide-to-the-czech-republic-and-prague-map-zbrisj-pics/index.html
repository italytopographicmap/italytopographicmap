FAQs about the Czech Republic and Prague Map:

1. What is the best way to get around Prague?

Public transportation, including trams, buses, and the metro, is an efficient and affordable way to navigate Prague. Walking is also a great option for exploring the city center and enjoying its charming streets.

2. What are some must-see attractions in Prague?

Prague Castle, Charles Bridge, Old Town Square, the Astronomical Clock, the Jewish Quarter, and the National Theatre are among the city’s most popular attractions.

3. How long should I spend in Prague?

A minimum of three days is recommended to experience Prague’s highlights. However, a week or more allows for a more in-depth exploration of the city and its surroundings.

4. What is the best time to visit Prague?

Spring and autumn offer pleasant weather and fewer crowds. Summer can be hot and crowded, while winter offers a magical Christmas atmosphere.

5. What is the currency used in the Czech Republic?

The Czech Republic uses the Czech koruna (CZK).
